# Strategic Growth Roadmap (Phase 3 Integration)

The platform is transitioning into **Phase 3: Digital Transformation & CRM**:

1.  **Digital QR Ordering Integration:** Mapping scanning URLs like `https://eatsonly.com/m/{slug}?t={table_id}` to trigger customer cart submissions and instantly dispatch Pusher/Reverb alerts to the waiter’s Flutter POS app.
2.  **Unified Multi-Channel pipelines:** Classifying transaction sources under `qr_self`, `pos_waiter`, `pos_counter`, and `web_delivery` to optimize analytics and kitchen workflows.
3.  **CRM Customer Database & Loyalty Engine:** Tracking customer visits and dynamically calculating loyalty point redemptions during billing checkouts.
